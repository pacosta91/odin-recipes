Ramen



Bowl of delicious ramen

Standard ramen made from pork bone stock, wheat noodles, pork, and egg.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Wheat noodles
  • Stock
  • Eggs
  • Mushrooms
  1. Simmer the stock with pork meat and bones overnight.
  2. Continue to simmer meat while cutting wheat noodles.
  3. Cook noodles to desired consistency.
  4. Boil eggs to desired level (usually soft-boiled is preferred).
  5. Serve hot in a bowl.
